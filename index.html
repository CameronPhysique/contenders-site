Welcome To CFM!

Contender Fighter Management provides commercial management services to both upcoming and established fighters. We approach each client with individual guidance and opportunities offering a bespoke service only available to a select few.



About Us

In a perfect world all aspiring or established combat athletes should be able to focus their time improving skills and developing future potential.

Regrettably, during the early stages of an athletes career, most fighters will have to work full-time to finance their progress. Sponsorship opportunities can be overlooked, or considered difficult for a developing athlete. We focus on deveoping commercial parntships so that you are free to coneentrate your time and attention on your physical progress.

Alternatively, for fighters who are established in their chosen discipline, and have a large following. We can help you leverage your influence to build mutually benificial sponsorships.

Contender Fighter Management can deliver! We work closely with partnered brands and established businesses on your behalf. We forge enduring partnerships and secure sponsorships that benefit the sponsor and the athlete equally.

We establish sponsorship opportunities within the combat sports industry, inlcuding grappling events, competitive interclub competitions, MMA shows, white collar boxing and alike.

The Process

Discovery

Schedule your discovery call with us today so we can learn about you, and how we can help you achieve your sponsorship & funding goals. Our team are waiting for your call.

Brand Identity

We will design your logo and brand identity that best suits your personality and your target audience. Strong branding enhances your identity and makes your more memorable.

Social Media Takeover

We will set up a shared media folder where you upload your images and videos. Our team will create professional social media content for athletes and brands to use during their campaigns. Our team will manage your social media, SEO, post scheduling, video editing and more!

Sponsorship

We design and customise a sponsorship pack for our athletes. Highlighting key data, accomplishments and competition schedule. We make it clear for sponsors to understand opportunities, providing businesses with ROI intel. Our approach is proactive rather than reactive, seeking and sourcing best suited brand partners for individual athletes.
